Who made Linux?

Linus Torvalds is the creater of the Linux kernel. This all started out as a hobby project in his time and for some time Torvalds was shy to put his work online for others to see. Later he did and decided to make it open-source, after that happened, thousands of programmers liked it and deiced to code for it and there the linux kernel was born.

What is linux now?

As you are reading this, Linux is being ran on NASA's international space ship, its being used to run the biggest servers of companies, its in all Android devices, it runs on almost all computers and the list can go on...
